Hidden Talent


Drawing and Painting

I'm not a professional artist but I have always enjoyed drawing and painting since young. It started off with my parents sending me for art lessons since young. Back then I was a grumpy child who disliked the crayons and watercolour classes because all I wanted was to watch Teen Titans. Who knew that this skill followed me throughout my childhood till my teenage years where I experimented with all forms of art - from sketching to colour pencils to poster colours and eventually digital art.

I helped out in my college with designing stickers and t-shirts for fundraising sales during Flag Day and when I am bored, I would end up drawing for at least 3 hours straight. I wouldn't say that I am very good at drawing but this is one of the skills that I'm better at I suppose.
