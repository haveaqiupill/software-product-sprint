Hidden Talent


Drawing and Painting

I'm not a professional artist but I have always enjoyed drawing and painting since young. It started off with my parents sending me for art lessons since young. Back then I was still a grumpy child who disliked the crayons and watercolour art classes that my parents signed me up for because all I wanted was to ... watch Teen Titans.

Who knew that this skill will follow me throughout my childhood till my teenage years where I experimented with all forms of art - from sketching to colour pencils to poster colours and eventually digital art.

The above designs were created when I helped out my college with designing stickers and t-shirts for fundraising sales during Flag Day. They represent the five houses in RC4 - Leo, Ursa, Draco, Noctua and Aquila. The Orca is the mascot that represents the college, as well as the colour Teal.


The picture above is a snapshot of my work-in-progress in drawing Vulpix. I usually have a reference (for colours and sketching) but I don't like to trace images as tracing a picture doesn't provide the same satisfaction as compared to drawing it from scratch. Adobe Photoshop is my go-to design software because of its versatility (although it heats up my poor Macbook Pro and significantly slows down my computer 🙁). I wouldn't say that I am extremely good at drawing but it has always been a platform for me to destress especially after long hours of studying.

When building websites and applications, I tend to involve myself in the designing of logos and theming of the websites (you can head over to the projects page to take a look at some of my designs).
